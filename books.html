    His Holiness Gohar Shahi wrote few books. Among these books "Religion of GOD" got much attention by Public. Religon Of God also reflects the teachings of His Holiness GOHAR Shahi.

    Religion-of-God

    English Version

    Religion of God includes the people who Love God. In this This book His Holiness Gohar Shahi teaches how Love of God enter in heart of human regard less of any religion.

    Deen e Ilaahi

    Urdu Version

    Deen e Ilaahi is the original book of his Holiness Gohar Shahi written in urdu Language. This book is the Ultimate source of guidance for those souls who love GOD.

    RoshNaas

    Roshnas is another book of His Holiness Gohar Shahi written in Urdu.

    Tariyaaq e Qalb (تریاق قلب)

    Tariyaaq e Qalb is the collection of His Holiness Gohar Shahi poetry.
