    What is the Religion of Gohar Shahi ? | Gohar Shahi ka Aqeeda

    Even though Gohar Shahi is Muslim Sufi personality but Gohar Shahi does not preach any religion instead He call people towards love of God through Spirituality. He says Love of God is the essence of every religion ,without love of God religions are useless.

    Gohar Says in His book Religion of God

    If your desire is to achieve paradise and its rewards, then worship extensively and strive to reach the highest levels of heaven. If you seek union with God, then learn spirituality and follow the Prescribed Divine Path. Through spirituality, you may attain the ultimate nearness and connection with God.
